Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our business consulting services.

What specific services does Netillia offer? expand_more

We specialize in business consulting for entrepreneurs and intrapreneurs in Nigeria. Our core services include Startup Support, Business Structure & Automation, Sales Training, and general SME development consulting to help you start, grow, and scale effectively.

How do I get started with a consultation? expand_more

Getting started is easy! You can fill out the contact form above, call our office directly, or reach out via WhatsApp. We'll schedule a discovery call to understand your specific business needs and propose a tailored plan.

Do you offer support specifically for startups in Nigeria? expand_more

Yes, absolutely. We have a deep understanding of the Nigerian market landscape. Our startup support services are designed to help new businesses navigate local regulatory requirements, market entry strategies, and operational setup.

Can you help automate my existing business processes? expand_more

Yes, Business Structure & Automation is one of our key areas of expertise. We help analyze your current workflow, identify bottlenecks, and implement digital tools and strategies to streamline your operations and reduce manual effort.

What is your pricing structure? expand_more

Our pricing is tailored to the specific needs and scope of each project. We offer various packages for consulting and training. Please contact us with your requirements for a customized quote.
