The Challenge

GOM Logistics was facing a critical bottleneck. Despite a growing fleet, their manual dispatch process resulted in lost orders, driver downtime, and increasing customer complaints. They needed to move from pen-and-paper tracking to a digital-first operation without disrupting daily business.

02

The Netillia Solution

We deployed a two-phase strategy focusing on Structure and Automation.

  • check_circle Workflow Audit: Mapped out every step of the delivery process to identify redundancy.
  • check_circle Custom Automation Tools: Implemented a low-code dispatch dashboard integrated with WhatsApp for drivers.
  • check_circle Capacity Building: Trained 3 managers and 25 drivers on the new digital tools.
03

The Outcome

Within 3 months, GOM Logistics saw a complete turnaround in operational stability, allowing the founder to step back from day-to-day firefighting.
